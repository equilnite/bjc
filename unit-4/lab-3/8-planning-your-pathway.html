    ## Jobs and Income
    <p>Many of the above communities share job listings with the public.</p>
    <div class="forYouToDo">
        <ol>
            <li>
                Find three job postings in computer science that look interesting to you. Write down:
                <ul>
                    <li>The job title</li>
                    <li>The qualitications required (degree, experience, special skills, etc.)</li>
                    <li>The salary</li>
                </ul>
            </li>
            <li></li>
        </ol>

    ## Beyond Being a Programmer
    <p>Computers and programming is important to careers far beyond software engineers.</p>
    <div class="forYouToDo">
        <ol>
            <li>Choose a career you are interested in, and research how computers are used on that field.</li>
            <li>Look into what kinds of specialized training in software packages and/or programming are relevant in that field.</li>

        </ol>
